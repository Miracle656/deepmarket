# Multi-Outcome Trading Guide

How to stake, trade, and redeem on a **multi-outcome** market (e.g. "Who wins
the 2026 World Cup?") end to end. Each outcome is its own tradable token; the
matching engine is the same on-chain order book (DeepBook V3) as the binary
markets — see [Multi-Outcome Markets](/products/multi-outcome-markets) for the
model.

<Callout type="info">
Open a market from the **Multi-Outcome Markets** section on `/markets`, or go
straight to `/outcome/<marketId>`. The page has four tabs: **Outcomes**,
**Chart**, **Order Book**, **Activity**.
</Callout>

## What you need

- A Sui wallet (e.g. **Slush**) with **testnet SUI** for gas and stakes.
- A little **DEEP** — only if you use the order book (limit orders). DeepBook
  charges fees in DEEP; the app auto-deposits a small amount per order.
- **Staking needs no DEEP** — the core mint/redeem flow is pure SUI.

## Two ways to take a position

<Steps>

### Stake (mint a fresh ticket)

In the right-hand ticket, pick an outcome → **Stake** tab → enter SUI → **Stake
on `<outcome>`**. You receive that outcome's token **1:1**. This is the
parimutuel mint — you back one outcome and get one token (unlike binary "Mint
Tokens", which gives a YES *and* a NO pair). No DEEP required.

### Trade on the order book (limit)

Switch to the **Limit ◆** tab to buy/sell an outcome at *your* price against
other traders. Price is **SUI per token, 0–1** — that's the implied probability
(`0.40` = 40%). Two sides:

- **Bid (buy)** — deposits `price × qty` SUI as collateral.
- **Ask (sell)** — deposits `qty` outcome tokens (so you must hold them — Stake
  first).

</Steps>

## Enabling the order book

<Callout type="warning">
**Do this once per outcome before your first order on it.** Permissionless
pools launch with no DEEP price reference, so the first order otherwise aborts
with `ENoDataPoints` (abort code 2).
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Enable trading (one-time per wallet)

If you've never traded on DeepMarket, the **Enable trading** banner (or button)
creates your DeepBook **BalanceManager** and seeds the live pools.

### Sync DEEP price (once per outcome)

In the **Limit ◆** tab, click **Sync DEEP price** before your first order on
that outcome. (If it says "already primed", you're good.)

### Post your order

Enter price + quantity → **Bid** or **Ask** → approve. The app rounds size to
the pool lot and deposits the DEEP fee for you.

</Steps>

## Reading the price

Each outcome shows a **headline %** and, when it has live trading, a smaller
**"N% staked"** with an info icon:

- **Headline %** = the live order-book mid (or last trade) — current market
  sentiment.
- **"N% staked"** = the outcome's share of the parimutuel pool — what the bar
  and your final payout are based on.

These two can differ, and the outcomes' headline %s **don't sum to 100** because
each trades in its own independent pool. That's expected — see the
[pricing notes](/products/multi-outcome-markets#pricing--probability).

## After a fill: claim your balances

Limit orders leave their collateral (and any filled proceeds) in your
**BalanceManager**. After an order fills, click **Claim filled balances** in the
Limit tab to sweep the bought tokens / sold SUI back to your wallet.

<Callout type="info">
You can watch your trades land under the **Activity** tab (price, size, time,
each linking to the transaction), and the resting orders under **Order Book**
(pick the outcome with the colored chips).
</Callout>

## Resolution & redeeming

Sports/discrete markets are resolved by the protocol admin (no price oracle).
Once resolved:

- The winning outcome's token redeems for a **pro-rata share of the whole
  pool**: `payout = your_tokens × vault / winning_supply`. Click **Redeem
  winning tokens**.
- Losing tokens are worth nothing.

<Callout type="warning">
**Cancel resting orders before resolution.** Resolving doesn't touch the order
book — open orders stay with collateral locked and can be picked off once the
winner is known. Cancel them first to reclaim your collateral.
</Callout>

## Differences from binary YES/NO markets

| | Binary (YES/NO) | Multi-outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Outcomes | 2 | 2–64 |
| Minting | "Mint Tokens" → YES **and** NO pair | "Stake" → the one outcome you pick |
| Pools | YES pool + NO pool | one pool **per outcome** |
| Redeem | winner pays **1:1** from vault | winner pays **pro-rata** share of the pool |
| Claim button | n/a (market orders auto-withdraw) | **Claim filled balances** (limit orders rest) |
